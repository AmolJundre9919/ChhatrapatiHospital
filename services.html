Diagnostic Services

Accurate diagnosis is the first step towards effective treatment

At Shri Chattrapati Shivaji Maharaj Hospital, we provide comprehensive diagnostic services using state-of-the-art equipment and technology. Our diagnostic department is staffed with experienced professionals who ensure accurate and timely results, enabling our doctors to provide the best possible treatment plans for our patients.

Our Diagnostic Facilities Include:

Laboratory Services

Comprehensive blood tests, urine analysis, and other pathological examinations.

Imaging Services

X-rays, Ultrasound, CT Scan, and MRI with detailed reports.

Cardiac Diagnostics

ECG, Echocardiography, Stress Tests, and Holter Monitoring.

Neurological Diagnostics

EEG, EMG, and Nerve Conduction Studies.

Our diagnostic services are available to both inpatients and outpatients. We offer quick turn-around times for test results, ensuring that treatment can begin promptly. For certain specialized tests, we have tie-ups with renowned reference laboratories to provide the most accurate results.

Treatment Services

Comprehensive care for various medical conditions

Our hospital offers a wide range of treatment services across various specialties. With a team of expert doctors and modern facilities, we are equipped to handle both simple and complex medical conditions. We follow evidence-based treatment protocols and personalize care plans based on individual patient needs.

Treatment Services We Offer:

Surgical Procedures

Advanced surgical facilities with minimally invasive techniques across specialties.

Medical Management

Comprehensive treatment for chronic and acute conditions using the latest medical protocols.

Physical Therapy

Rehabilitation services to help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, or chronic conditions.

Interventional Procedures

Non-surgical procedures like angioplasty, stenting, and catheterization.

Our treatment approach focuses on holistic care, considering both the physical and emotional well-being of our patients. We have dedicated inpatient wards, ICUs, and operation theaters equipped with advanced technology to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

Preventive Healthcare

Prevention is better than cure

At Shri Chattrapati Shivaji Maharaj Hospital, we believe in the importance of preventive healthcare. Our preventive services aim to detect potential health issues before they become serious and to promote overall wellness through regular check-ups, screenings, and lifestyle guidance.

Our Preventive Healthcare Services Include:

Health Check-up Packages

Comprehensive health assessment packages tailored for different age groups and risk profiles.

Vaccination Services

Complete range of vaccinations for children and adults as per standard guidelines.

Wellness Programs

Programs focusing on diet, exercise, stress management, and lifestyle modification.

Cancer Screening

Regular screenings for common cancers to detect and treat them at early stages.

Our preventive healthcare services are designed to empower individuals to take charge of their health. Regular check-ups and screenings can help identify risk factors and early signs of disease, allowing for timely intervention and better outcomes.

Emergency Services

24/7 care for critical situations

Our emergency department is operational 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to provide immediate care for critical and life-threatening conditions. We have a team of emergency medicine specialists, supported by other specialists on call, to handle any medical emergency efficiently.

Our Emergency Services Include:

Trauma Care

Immediate attention to accident victims and trauma patients.

Cardiac Emergency

Specialized care for heart attacks and other cardiac emergencies.

Stroke Management

Quick assessment and treatment for stroke patients.

Ambulance Services

Well-equipped ambulances with trained paramedics for quick transportation.

Our emergency department is equipped with advanced life support systems, monitoring equipment, and quick access to diagnostic services. We follow a triage system to prioritize patients based on the severity of their condition, ensuring that critical cases receive immediate attention.

Specialized Care

Expert care for specific medical needs

We offer specialized medical services across various departments to address specific health conditions and needs. Our specialists have extensive training and experience in their respective fields, ensuring that patients receive expert care for complex conditions.

Our Specialized Services Include:

Cardiac Care

Advanced cardiac procedures including angioplasty, pacemaker implantation, and cardiac rehabilitation.

Neurology Services

Treatment for neurological disorders, stroke management, and neurosurgical procedures.

Orthopedic Procedures

Joint replacements, spine surgeries, sports injury management, and rehabilitation.

Maternal and Child Care

Comprehensive obstetric and gynecological services, neonatal care, and pediatric treatments.

Our specialized care units are equipped with advanced medical technology and staffed by multidisciplinary teams. We follow a patient-centered approach, ensuring that each individual receives personalized care based on their specific condition and needs.

Need Medical Assistance?

Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to providing you with the best medical care. Whether you need a consultation, a specialized treatment, or emergency services, we are here to help.
